• ng-repeat iterates over a collection of items
  • Each template gets its own scope, with following added properties:
    • $index: iterator offset of the repeated element.
    • $first , $middle, $last: booleans used to test the element position in the collection.
<!-- div ng-repeat="user in listUsers" --> <div ng-repeat="login in [ 'Bob', 'Luc', 'Bill' ]"> <b>{{login}}</b>. Index: {{$index}}, first: {{$first}}, middle: {{$middle}}, last: {{$last}} </div>
